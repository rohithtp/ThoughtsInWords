 Here's a step-by-step guide for Indian citizens on how to apply for a Singapore tourist or business visa in 2026:

1. When to Apply: It is recommended to apply at least 7–10 days before your travel date, though you can apply up to 30 days in advance.

2. Where to Apply: You cannot apply directly through government portals. Instead, you must use an authorized Singapore visa agent or have a local contact in Singapore (citizen, permanent resident, or work pass holder) apply on your behalf.

3. Processing Time: Visas typically take 3–5 working days to process.

4. Required Documents:
   - Original passport (with at least 6 months validity)
   - Completed Form 14A
   - Recent passport-sized photographs (per ICA guidelines)
   - A cover letter
   - Supporting documents depending on your status (salaried employees need salary slips and bank statements, business owners need ITR/GST filings, and those visiting friends/family require an invitation letter or Form V39A).

5. Six-Step Application Process:
   1. Select an authorized ICA-approved agent.
   2. Complete Form 14A (often assisted by your agent).
   3. Submit all required documents.
   4. Pay the visa fee (official fee is 30 SGD, plus agent service charges, totaling under 3,500 INR).
   5. Wait for consulate processing.
   6. Receive your e-visa via email and verify all details.

6. Common Pitfalls & Tips:
   - Reasons for rejection: Low bank balance, incomplete forms, blurred document scans, or suspicious travel history.
   - Best practices: Maintain a bank balance of at least 50,000–1,00,000 INR, avoid booking non-refundable tickets until the visa is approved, and always carry a printed copy of your e-visa when traveling.
